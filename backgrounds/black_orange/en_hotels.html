1. ASTORIA CAPSIS HOTEL


4 star Hotel in the heart of Heraklion in Liberty Square. It has three specially adapted rooms on the 4th floor, suitable for wheelchairs width of less than 75cm. At the entrance you will find a step 21m. but the hotel has a mobile ramp. Will struggle with the height of the reception counter. WC room: it has handles and shower without step.

The hotel has two meeting rooms accessible (level B). For the Snack Bar will need the portable ramp. There are no parking spaces but cooperate with outside parking. The customer leaves IX of the entrance and parked ones.

Liberty Square 11, Heraklion Crete

Phone number: 2810-343080 / 2810-343082

Links: http://www.capsishotels.gr/el/index.html
http://www.astoriacapsis.gr/el/index.html

2. APOLLONIA BEACH RESORT & SPA


Hotel with very good access, large common areas, and ramps in the various sections. Accepts group disabled and no relevant experience. Has 10 specially designed rooms (8 bungalows and 2 in the main building), suitable for wheelchairs width less than 79 cm. Ta bungalows are accessible by paved road. The door of the WC (room) opens inward but there is enough free space. The shower has a step projecting 3cm. There is beach access with a wooden ramp that goes near the sea.

Hotel facilities: restaurant (in the garden), bar, conference hall, swimming pools, beach all accessible. You will find 2 shared specially adapted WC. The first in the pool snack bar near the beach. The second -1 level of the building (elevator door 69cm.). Caution is needed in small marble protruding thresholds at the entrance WC, in the rooms and some ramps are relatively steep.

Near the hotel is the center hemodialysis Messogios. The Spa is located in a separate building across the street. There is a special infrastructure of people with disabilities in specially adapted WC, access ramps, medical-physiotherapy staff.

Ammoudara Gazi

Phone number: 2810-821624/2810-821602

Links: http://www.apollonia.gr/

3. HOTEL AGAPI BEACH


Located in the coastal area of Ammoudara. There are two specially adapted bungalows in the outer open space (ground floor) 150 meters from the reception area, accessible with some little steep ramps.

The hotel is suitable for wheelchairs width of less than 77cm. WC Rooms have handles, shower (not protruding), but there is not enough space between the sanitary ware. There are hypoallergenic rooms. The hotel has a restaurant, pool, fitness center all accessible. For the garden and the pool will need help (2 small steps 10cm.).

There is beach access path to the bungalows while the convention center is adjusted ramp in three steps of the entrance.

N. Stadium, Heraklion

Phone number: 2810-250502

Links: http://www.agapibeach.gr

4. MEGARON LUXURY HOTEL


Built in 1925 and classified as a historical monument, the GDM Megaron luxury hotel is located in the heart of Heraklion, overlooking the harbor. It has 2 specially adapted rooms on the 1st and 2nd floor. The hotel is suitable for wheelchairs width less than 79ek.Me Chair will enter the alternative entrance from the harbor (street Ephesus) because the main entrance has stairs. The door of the WC (room) opens inward but has enough free space.

There are handles and shower without step. Problem will meet in the basin because it is buried in recess (only frontal approach) and the sink because it is furniture. Hotel facilities: gym, spa, roof garden, conference room, café, restaurant but some of them will be hard because you will find doors 68 cm and small steps. There is a specially designed communal WC.

D.Mpofor 9 Heraklion

Phone number: 2810-305300

Links: http://www.gdmmegaron.gr/

5. ROYAL MARE VILLAGE


There are 8 adapted rooms in the main building, 1st floor (elevator door 76 cm). In other areas there is excellent access. The hotel has a central bar with lounge, 2 main pools and dozens of other smaller, all easily accessible from outdoor ramps network and corridors.

Non-smoking rooms and dietician and special menus. Even you will find accessible conference center and accessible center Spa and Thalassotherapy "Thalasso" (autonomous) which are accessible physiotherapy programs for People with Disabilities (may be accompanied by their physiotherapist).
